Code of Conduct

For our hackathons to take place smoothly and safely, WeHack requires all participants to adhere to our Code of Conduct:

Do

  • Always be friendly, harmonious, and polite with everyone.
  • Respect the Judges, Advisory Boards, and Sponsors; without their help, there would be no meaningful hackathons for all of us.
  • Show respect to each individual inside and outside the group.
  • Complete the assigned tasks following the rules and deadlines given by the hackathon.
  • Actively and confidently participate in hackathons activities.
  • Develop creativity by yourself and the whole team in the spirit of solidarity

Don't

  • Discrimination, disrespect, harassment, and impolite behavior towards other group members.
  • Be disrespectful towards the Jury, Advisory Board, Sponsors, and Organizers.
  • Physically and mentally abusive behavior towards other members through various forms.
  • Using negative language and images in the process of participating and creating products in various forms.
  • Failure to comply with the regulations of the organizer.
  • Plagiarize existing ideas and products on social networking sites and many other forms.
  • Hackathon participants who violate our Code of Conduct will be warned, sanctioned, or expelled from the hackathon at the organizer's discretion.
